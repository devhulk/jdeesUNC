Watsky The Enigmatic.

Bio

Watsky is a rapper hailing from California, his career began with his spoken word poetry. In 2007 he put out his first album "Invisible Inc." Ever since then he has steadily released a stream of critically acclaimed music, and garnered fans across the globe. To date he has gone on two world tours, and has had multiple tours around the United States.
